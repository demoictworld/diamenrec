SCENARIO
Scenario earned his name from high school friends who noticed the rapper's ability to naturally point out the "Worst Case Scenario's" in situations that felt uncomfortable. Finally admitting to the claims, he accepted the name as his alias and would use it to "create scenarios" through his music. On January 1st 1989, Etobicoke General Hospital, Scenario was the first baby to be born that year. Being featured in the local newspaper, this was Scenario's first introduction to the public eye. It would take 10 years before he realized his dreams and gifts as a rapper and song writer.
At the age of 10, scenario was inspired by the work ethic of Sean "Diddy" Combs. Not only did he take interest in the artistic side of the industry, the business side drew him in as well. Now, 12 years later even more eager, he is determined to make a name for himself. Surrounding him self with a strong team, true friends and supportive parents, Scenario is driven to be an entertainer the world can relate to. Some of his influences for both Artistic value and business ethic would include: "Diddy", Jay-Z, Mathew Knowles, Drake and Lil Wayne. Believing many GTA artists are working towards a common goal, Scenario helps others along on their own musical journey. Writing lyrics for other groups and collaborating with other artist, Scenario will soon release his newest mix tape "Executive Decision". And with two promotional music videos he shows his fans growth over the years. Scenario feels as though he is ready to work professionally. Scenarios mantra: "I'm not there yet, but I'll do it like I'm close enough".
